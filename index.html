About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Tong Zhang starting from Sep 2019. Before my Ph.D journey, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University. I also have the prividge to work with Prof. Zhiyuan Liu during my undergraduate study.

My current research interest is Sparse Neural Networks, Efficient Training/Inference, OOD generalziation, etc.
